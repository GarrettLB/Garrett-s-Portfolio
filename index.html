About me

Here's a few facts about me: My name is Garrett. I am currently 22 years old and I live in a Metro-Atlanta city in Georgia named Marietta. I graduated early from highschool at 16 and took a 2 year Welding and Joining Technologies course at Chattahoochee Tech at 17. In my spare time I pursue weight lifting and sports. And although not very physichal like my other interests, I've always been interested in computers!

My last several jobs over the years have all been in the contruction feild. I've held several different positions, with a wide range from general construction to grading to framing to welding to electrical. But it's time for a change! I started self-learning how to code and fell in love with it. I liked it so much I even enrolled in GA Tech's Full Stack Coding Bootcamp! I'm enjoying the journey of learning and am eager for my first job in the feild.
